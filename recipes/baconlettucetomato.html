Bacon Lettuce and Tomato

Cant go wrong with bacon

This sandwitch is crispy crunchy and savory. What more can you want?

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Place bacon in the frying pan at a high heat
  2. When the bacon is crisp grab the tongs and put the bacon on a plate
  3. Wash the lettuce and tomato
  4. Cut the desired amount of tomato in to slices
  5. Cut the desired amount of lettuce
  6. Take two slices of bread and apply the desired amount of mayonaise
  7. Now that the bacon is cooled place the lettuce, tomato and bacon on to the bread and close like a book
  8. Enjoy your memoir
