Kinilaw

Marinated and delicious

This recipe takes fresh tuna and instead of cooking we marinate in coconut vinegar

Ingredients

  • Fresh tuna steak
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger
  • Chiles
  • Coconut vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash your tomato and dice
  2. Wash two chiles and dice
  3. Peel and dice a knob of ginger
  4. Peel and dice your onion
  5. Cut your tuna steak in to bite sized cubes
  6. Place your tuna in a bowl
  7. Pour coconut vinegar in to the bowl so that most of the tuna is submerged
  8. Squeeze the tuna to suck up the coconut vinegar like a sponge
  9. Get a serving bowl and add half a cup of coconut vinegar along with the diced ingredients
  10. Discard the vinegar from the bowl with the tuna
  11. Add tuna to the serving bowl and mix
  12. Enjoy
