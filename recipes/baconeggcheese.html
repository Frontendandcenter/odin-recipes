Bacon Egg and Cheese

Breakfast of champions

This sometimes underestimated meal is a quick and easy breakfast and one of my favorites.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Herbs & Spices
  • Croissant

Steps

  1. Put the desired amount of bacon in the frying pan on high heat
  2. When the bacon is crisp set the heat to medium and put the bacon on a plate
  3. Crack the desired abount of eggs in the pan with the bacon fat
  4. Season with herbs and spices my favorites are oregano, thyme, garlic powder and chili flakes
  5. When the yoke hardens flip the egg and put cheese on top followed by the bacon
  6. Cut the croissant in half and place the bacon egg and cheese in the middle
  7. Enjoy
